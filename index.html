    Peppy Tomato
    Vegetable Roll

    5

    10 min

    Ingredients:

    Saffola Peppy Tomato Oats – 40 g
    Saffola Active oil – 2 tbsp 5 ml
    Cumin seeds – 1 tsp 2 g
    Carrot – ½ cup 100 g
    Capsicum & Onion – ½ cup 50 g each
    Spinach Tortilla - 2 x 100 g

    Method:

    1. Follow the directions to cook Saffola Peppy Tomato Oats and set them aside to cool
    2. Heat Saffola Active Oil in a pan and add cumin seeds, capsicum, onion, salt to taste, and black pepper. Cook for 3-4 minutes on a low flame
    3. Keep a spinach tortilla on a plate and spread some oats on top.
